What is The Green Shift?

Facing climate challenges, The Green Shift offers hope by combining funding, innovation, and collective action for sustainability. Billions invested are leading to advancements in various sectors.

Renewable Progress

In a decade, renewable energy costs plummeted: wind became three times cheaper, and solar dropped to a tenth. Despite heavy fossil fuel subsidies, renewables like wind and solar now outcompete traditional power plants, marking a hopeful transition to a sustainable, cost-effective energy future.

Is it enough?

Over the past decade, solar energy production soared 25 times and wind five times. While this growth is impressive, it introduces challenges, especially with storing all this energy.
